Future Talks provides a platform to discuss and exchange knowledge about topics of today and the future.
While there are many meetups and groups targeted at specific user groups, we encourage a broader spectrum of topics, focusing computer scientists.
We usually meet on each first sunday of the month to learn from each others, co-create and hang out.
Previous sessions have already covered topics like AI, Blockchain, AR/VR, Cloud computing, JS Framworks and Libraries and there is more to come, stay tuned.

Seit dem 02.12.2018 ist "Future Talks" offiziell ein Verein.
